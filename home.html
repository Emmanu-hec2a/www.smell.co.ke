About Us

Excellence in Engineering Since 2014

San Marina Engineering Limited (SMEL) is a leading multidisciplinary engineering firm specializing in electrical engineering, renewable energy solutions, and environmental services. Since our establishment in 2014, we have built a reputation for delivering innovative, sustainable, and reliable solutions across Kenya.

Our team of highly qualified engineers and technicians combines technical expertise with a commitment to quality and safety, ensuring every project meets the highest standards. We are proud holders of EPRA Class A1 License and NCA Category 5 certification, demonstrating our capability to handle diverse and complex engineering projects.
